I started kitesurfing in 2004 on Lake Poland, teaching myself with the help of my father. From the very beginning, I became addicted to the sport and developed my skills in all styles, including freestyle, wave riding, big air, and kite foil. These days, I am particularly passionate about kite foil. Over the years, I have worked as a kiteboard tester, managed a kitesurfing school, and even had the opportunity to create my own school in Tarifa. Additionally, I spent several years developing a kitesurfing app. However, the greatest happiness and joy I have found is on the beach, teaching kitesurfing directly to people as an instructor. Throughout my career, I have taught hundreds of independent kitesurfers of all levels and styles. I am passionate about sharing my love for the sport and helping others achieve their kitesurfing goals. Join me on the beach and let's ride the wind together!
Currently, I work at Alex Pastor Kiteclub, a kite school located in Tarifa, the mecca of water sports. Here, I work with high-quality Airush equipment. If you want to enhance your kitesurfing or wingfoil skills, or perhaps try one of these exhilarating water sports, you can reserve a kitesurfing or wingfoil lesson with me. I will do my utmost to teach you and share my vast experience with a smile!
