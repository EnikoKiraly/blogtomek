I started meditating when I was around 14 years old, during my first yoga and aikido lessons. Since then, I have enjoyed exploring different meditation techniques and breathing practices. However, the biggest game-changer for me was my trip to Asia in 2018. While surfing in Bali, I also traveled to Thailand for a while, where I attended my first Vipassana retreat for 12 days, meditating between 8-20 hours daily. After the retreat, I have meditated every day without missing a single day. Meditation has been incredibly helpful for me in managing my emotions, and I feel more present and focused every day. I always try to be helpful to people who are interested in discovering meditation. I practice various techniques, including sitting, lying down, and walking meditation. The technique I use is very simple and involves observing the breath - rising and falling.
